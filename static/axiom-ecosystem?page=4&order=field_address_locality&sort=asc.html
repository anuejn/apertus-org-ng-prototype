AXIOM Ecosystem

Introduction

AXIOM ecosystem acts as a platform to offer individual services and development in the community. Another primary purpose for this ecosystem is to collect feedback from the community with a view to subsequently develop and improve cinema camera systems further in the future.



No license fees & No royalties

As the AXIOM and apertus° philosophies are built on a fundamental Open Hardware and Free Software approach there are no license restrictions limiting the ecosystem. Anyone can offer services or products in the AXIOM ecosystem and there's no obligation for them to pay royalties.
