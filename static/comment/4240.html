Phase 1 Report & AXIOM Beta Production Status

Submitted by Sebastian on Wed, 03/02/2016 - 16:25

The Phase 1 for the AXIOM Beta has been completed in the first week of February and in total we received the funds of 123 Phase 1 payments (unfortunately not all 500 crowdfunding camera supporters followed through). This will not allow us to get the 500 pieces volume deals, but the 100+ volume order will still give us quite significant discounts for a number of key items - such as the sensor, which is by far the most expensive single component. The estimated price increase of the at-cost prices for those 123 backers should be quite small (see our previous price estimate). Also we will try our best to save cost in other areas to further reduce the at-cost prices.

Production Status

We have successfully completed the production of the first 5 AXIOM Beta developer kits. Two cameras are used for in house development and testing and 3 have been provided to core developers (like Magic Lantern). As every electronic component is currently picked and placed on the boards by hand, the effort required to build one AXIOM Beta from start to finish is around 1 man week. We will produce around 15 - 20 developer kits in total for those backers who expressed interest in receiving an early adopter kit. If you are a Phase 1 backer and have not expressed interest in getting your hardware early in a past survey, please contact us.
